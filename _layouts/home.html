The Real Effects of Perceptual Errors: Evidence from M&As

with Eliezer Fich, 2nd round Revise and Resubmit at Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis

Industry-peers' earnings surprises announced a day before the M&A announcements correlate with the acquirers’ M&A announcement return. Biased reactions due to salience can best explain our findings. We show that the salience effects distort the M&A bidding process.

Conferences: AFA, Research in Behavioral Finance Conference
